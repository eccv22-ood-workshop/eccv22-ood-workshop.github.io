Challenge Instruction

Dataset

The goal of our OOD-CV benchmark is do diagnose robustness of computer vision models to out-of-distribution shifts in the data. OOD-CV is a dataset that consists of a training set with 10 object categories (aeroplane, bus, car, train, boat, bicycle, motorplane, chair, dining table, sofa) from the PASCAL VOC 2012 and ImageNet datasets. The test set is designed such that each test example is subject to an out-of-distribution shift in one specific nuisance, such as the object's 3D pose, shape, texture, context, the weather, and occlusion.

Download

Train and Validation set can be downloaded from here. The final test set will not be publicly available (see details of the evaluation process below).

Evaluation

The final test set will not be publicly available for competitors to download, but it will instead be hosted on a Codalab server. Competitors are asked to upload their trained models via Codalab, which will then be tested on the private test set. In this way we aim to ensure that the test set will not be overfittet during the competition.

Submission

We expect the Codalab submission site to be online by the end of June. Until then, the competitors can evlauate their models on the publicly availabele validation set.

Prizes

The top-performing teams will be invited to publish in an IJCV special issue. There will also be cash prizes for the top-performing teams.
