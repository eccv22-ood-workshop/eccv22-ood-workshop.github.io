Call for Papers

We invite submissions of both long and short papers on the topic of out-of-distribution generalization. The long papers should be formatted in the ECCV 2022 format and should be the same length (max 14 pages) as ECCV 2022 papers. The short papers should be within 4 pages, and can use the format of CVPR templates. The topics include but are not limited to:
  • Improving generalization of computer vision systems in OOD scenarios
  • Research at the intersection of biological and machine vision
  • Generative causal models for image analysis
  • Domain generalization
  • Novel architectures with robustness to occlusion, viewpoint and other real-world domain shifts
  • Domain adaptation techniques for robust vision system in the real world
  • Datasets for evaluating model robustness

Please submit you paper to the Submission Website.
